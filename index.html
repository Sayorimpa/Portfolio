A Journey in Design

With a background in Microbiology and professional training in design, I combine analytical thinking with creativity to craft meaningful digital experiences. My portfolio reflects this transition — showcasing user-centered app redesigns, UI/UX case studies, and visual design explorations that highlight both functionality and aesthetics.

I focus on creating clean, intuitive, and engaging solutions that balance usability with visual appeal. Alongside UI/UX work, I also explore digital painting, logo design, packaging mockups, posters, and ad concepts — adding creative diversity to my process. Each of these projects complements my primary goal: building thoughtful, research-driven user experiences that bring ideas to life.
